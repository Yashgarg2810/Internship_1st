About our project

Introducing Sejahtera Residence

SEJAHTERA RESIDENCE
A Twenty-five apartment units Vila | Total Build up GFA: 27,708 Sq Ft | Expected T.O.P date: Q3 2025

A Twenty-five apartment units Villa

Consists of one five-storey residential block, with roof top swimming pool, a function room, a gym, basement carparks and a roof top vegetation garden and a wellness garden on the ground floor.

Modern with Italian influenced interior design

The Villa reflects Italian minimalist and functional design, using premium materials sourced directly from Italy to enhance its quality and market value.

Sustainability emphasized

The Villa will be designed and equipped with renewable energy sources and the capability to harvest rain water for non-potable use, promoting sustainable living with minimal environmental impact and long-term resource efficiency.

Our Investment Scheme

EQUITY INVESTORS (EI).

  • It is a program where the Developer invites select individuals or firms to participate as Equity Investors (EI) in real estate project.
  • EIs become part of the Project Company with an ownership stake, sharing both the risks and profits of the development. They have early access to project insights, budgets, and timelines.
  • EIs benefit from priority allocation and higher ROI potential, including profit-sharing upon project completion or unit sales.
LEARN MORE ABOUT EIS AND INVESTMENT OPTIONS →

CO-DEVELOP BUYERS (CDB).

  • It is a scheme whereby the Developer invites prospective buyers to be Co-Develop Buyers (CDB).
  • CDBs join to the Project Company as equity investor and co-developers and shareholders. They will pre-select a unit of their choice with the first right of refusal to that unit during the sales launch.
  • CDBs get to buy their selected unit at almost cost price, between 8–12% off the official launch price.
